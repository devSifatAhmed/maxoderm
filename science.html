Why Does Maxoderm Have a Special Scent?



The majority of our customers enjoy the light, fresh, wintergreen scent of Maxoderm. It’s very important to us that our customers understand the purpose behind this special scent.

a. Potency. The scent in Maxoderm comes from one of the chief ingredients, Methyl Salicylate. Methyl Salicylate is one of the ingredients responsible for enhanced sensation. When we first formulated Maxoderm, over twenty-five years ago, the scent was much stronger than it is today. We’ve tested numerous formulas varying the levels of Methyl Salicylate (and in some cases replacing it), but all formulas were just too weak for production. However, we were successful in lessening the scent to a minimum without negatively affecting the potency.

b. Imitators Can’t Fool Patented Formulation. Maxoderm’s formula is so unique it’s been issued a US Patent by the US Patent and Trademarking Office. Because the scent of Methyl Salicylate is so distinctive, the absence of that scent is a surefire giveaway that the lotion you’re using is an imitator and not the real deal.

c. Arousal by Association. Many of our Maxoderm users have no worries over the light scent of Maxoderm. In fact, some of them feel the smell help them get aroused – especially if they’re a long time user. It’s a well-known scientific fact that smells assist in memory and when you Maxoderm is right for you – the scent alone may actually help you get more “excited”.
